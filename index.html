About Us

At Blob Records, we bring your music to life on vinyl exactly how you envision it. Every record is tailored to the artist's preferences, from sound quality to visual details.

For those who care about the planet as much as the music, we offer recycled vinyl options. Old records are carefully melted and re-pressed, creating stunning, one-of-a-kind color patterns—so every pressing is truly unique. By giving vinyl a second life, we reduce waste and help make your music journey more sustainable.

Experience the perfect harmony of artistry and eco-consciousness—because great music deserves a greener footprint.
